# Business Overview: Besimplit

## 1. Core Identity
- **Website:** [besimplit.com](https://besimplit.com)
- **Tagline:** "Obras más rentables. Costos bajo control. Flota operativa."
- **Mission:** Transform the management of construction and related industries through a simple, effective integrated platform.
- **Location:** Santiago, Chile.

## 2. Product/Platform
Besimplit is an integrated management solution that connects production, costs, and machinery data in real-time.

### Key Modules:
- **Cost Control:** Tracks overhead and direct costs to identify profitable items.
- **Fuel Control (Bediesel):** Real-time monitoring of fuel consumption with anomaly detection.
- **Maintenance Control:** Centralizes preventive and corrective maintenance for machinery.
- **Production Control:** Monitors progress and payment statuses remotely.
- **Machinery Profitability:** Compares performance and maximizes ROI for equipment.

### Technical Highlights:
- **Bediesel App:** Mobile app for field data entry without office re-typing.
- **Offline Mode:** Works without internet and syncs when back online.
- **Traceability:** 24/7 monitoring and audit trails.
- **Dashboards:** Specialized data visualization for real-time decision-making.

## 3. Target Markets
- **Construction:** Site management and operational excellence.
- **Machine Rental:** Profitability tracking per unit and investment decisions.
- **Aggregates & Quarries:** Operational cost management and productivity monitoring.

## 4. Business Model & Pricing
- **Control de Faenas (Site Control):** 4 UF + IVA per site/month.
- **Control de Máquinas (Machine Control):** 0.3 UF + IVA per machine/month.
- **Go-to-Market:** Demo-based sales funnel ([app.besimplit.com](https://app.besimplit.com)).

## 5. Culture & Values
- **Mantenlo Simple (Keep it Simple):** User-friendly technology and non-bureaucratic collaboration.
- **Haz que Suceda (Make it Happen):** Proactive attitude and commitment to results.
- **Mejorar Juntos (Improve Together):** Emphasis on collaboration between the team and clients.
